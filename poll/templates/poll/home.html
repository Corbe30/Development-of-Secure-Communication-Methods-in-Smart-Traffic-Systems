Blockchain Voting Flow Details

1. A new vehicle which joins the network will take a copy of the ledger and blockchain.
2. The vehicle will vote and share the ledger and blockchain with everyone. Everyone verifies the last block with last transaction when they receive the block. The verification conditions are:
 1. Adder incurs a loss of 10%
 2. Adder isn’t voting twice
 3. Check for tampering

 [ in case of multiple blockchains, choose block with greater deduction of reputation in last block ]

3. At each vote, two smart contracts are executed in the same order:
 1. One for checking if it is the [VehicleCap]th vote. If so:
  1. Calculates the verdict from votes by referring the ledger [by using stake+probability+vote]
  2. add x blocks in the network - where x is the number of winners. Award 48% of current amount to the winners. Award 55% to the creator of the event.
 2. Other for incurring voting cost of 10% of reputation


Consensus

We are using Proof-of-reputation consensus. In this, nodes must first establish their reputation by proving their identity. In our project, a vehicle proves their identity by validating itself with the RTA and RSU. Reputation score is calculated based on a node’s historical behavior in the network. Each voter puts 10% of their reputation at stake, so everyone can put a block in the blockchain. Other nodes validate the latest block:
1. Isn’t double voting
2. has put atleast 10% of its reputation at stake

Data in a Transaction

- Event name
- Location and time
- Creator’s public key
- Voter’s public key [ = creator in case of first vote ]
- Voter’s final reputation after vote / result
